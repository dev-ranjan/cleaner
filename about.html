About Us

Welcome to OverflowAlert, your go-to platform for tackling overflowing dustbins in your area! We understand the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy environment, and we're here to empower individuals like you to take action.

Our Mission

At OverflowAlert, our mission is clear: to address the issue of overflowing dustbins and promote a cleaner, greener environment. We believe that by providing a platform for community engagement and action, we can make a tangible difference in our surroundings.

How It Works

Our platform is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. Simply sign up and start clicking pictures of overflowing dustbins in your area using our mobile app or website. Your contributions will help raise awareness about the problem and prompt action from local authorities.

Why Choose Us

  • Community-driven: We believe in the power of community action. By coming together, we can amplify our impact and bring about positive change.
  • Convenience: Our platform is designed to be accessible to everyone. With just a few clicks, you can report overflowing dustbins and play your part in keeping your surroundings clean.
  • Transparency: We're committed to transparency in everything we do. From the reporting process to the actions taken by authorities, you can trust us to keep you informed every step of the way.
